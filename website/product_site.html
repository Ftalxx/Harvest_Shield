product

Harvest Shield™ is your farm's ultimate ally against pests and diseases. By combining advanced image recognition, AI-driven predictions, and IoT sensor data, we offer a comprehensive solution for early detection, prevention, and treatment. Protect your crops, optimize yields, and ensure sustainable farming practices. Below are the main features of our product:

  • Computer Vision

    Early detection of diseases and pests reduces the need for harmful pesticides, safeguarding both the environment and consumer health. Our advanced image analysis technology enables precise identification, leading to timely interventions and optimized crop management.

  • AI-Powered Predictions

    Harnessing the power of artificial intelligence, Harvest Shield™ offers predictive analytics to anticipate potential threats. Stay ahead of crop challenges and make informed decisions to protect your investment.

  • Efficient Remediation​

    Precisely target affected areas, minimizing chemical usage. Reduce environmental impact and protect water sources with our targeted treatment approach.

  • GPS Tracking

    Precise field mapping enables farmers to identify areas with specific crop health issues or varying soil conditions. This targeted approach optimizes resource allocation and ensures that every part of the field receives the attention it needs.
